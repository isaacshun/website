FRED-DB

FRED-DB is a bot I developed using discord.py and fredapi that allows users to retrive data from the Federal Reserve Economic Data Database and plot it for analysis. I notice that there are finance discord servers where they have to livestream every time they want to discuss economic data and charts with others. So with this, they can just discuss and talk through chat.
